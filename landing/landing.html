Project

CarToFix is a complete Workshop Manager System to know every detail of each service at all times You can register each vehicle that arrives to your workshop: trade, model, plate, color, and every observation your technician needs to save to share all the information with the whole team.

Know the Features

Know how many vehicles you are working in at everytime, filter them by specialization to know how many more you can accept, and work without surprises. CarToFix will help you know your workshop better than ever before. You can automatically send an email to your client once the budget is ready, he just has to make a click and approve it to start the job. Every vehicle will have his technical historial so the entire team can easily track any faults that may have occurred in the past and take them into account in future repairs. You can give pencil and paper a vacation, Cartofix is ​​here so that in your workshop, you can only deal with repairs.
